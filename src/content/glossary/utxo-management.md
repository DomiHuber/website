# UTXO Management

UTXO management refers to the strategy and handling of multiple unspent transaction outputs within a wallet. Efficient UTXO management reduces transaction fees, avoids unnecessary address reuse, and minimizes privacy leaks. It also ensures that inputs are selected in a way that balances performance, cost, and long-term usability of the UTXO set.
